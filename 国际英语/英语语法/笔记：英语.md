## 主干部分

谓语的三态一否一一致（情态、时态、语态、否定、一致）

（1）情态：you must + V, you can + V, you will + V, ...
（2）语态：被动语态：be动词+Ved

![[image_20240917213336.png]]



## 主干-时态-一般现在时

1、V要么走be动词路线，要么实义动词，两者互斥。

（1）I am a student. 表示常态或者经常发生.

be动词句式：
肯定句：I am your friend.
否定句：I am not your friend.  （系动词+not）
一般疑问句：Am I your friend?  （系动词放句首）
特殊疑问句：Who is your friend?  (特殊疑问句 = 特殊疑问词 + 一般疑问句)
祈使句：祈使句就是把主语给省略了，往往主语是you。
祈使句例句：Be my sister. 我的好姐妹，你是我的好姐妹。

肯定句：主语 + V(s) + 其他  （He loves she.）
否定句：主语 + don't/doesn't + V + 其他  （He doesn't love she.）
一般疑问句：Do/Does + 主语 + V + 其他  （Does he love she?）
特殊疑问句：特殊疑问词 + 一般疑问句  （Why does he love she?）


```cpp
A is B --> A is who? --> who is A?
A is B --> Who is B?
```



【错误】You are love me.
【错误】You not love me.
【正确】You don't love me.
【错误】You are not love me.
错误原因：be动词和实义动词，它们是互斥的，不能同时出现。


## 主干-时态-一般过去时

样式（一般过去时）\
（1）was, were    【我曾是xxx】\
（2）V(ed)    同样与be动词互斥。【我曾做了xxx】


例子：I was a student last year. 我去年曾是一名学生。（现在不是了）
例子：I loved you. 我曾爱过你。（现在不爱了）
例子：He didn't love she. 他从没爱过她.
例子：Did he love she? 他爱她吗?
例子:  Why did he love she? 他为什么爱她?


## 主干-时态-进行时


样式（现在进行时）：
（1）am/is/are + Ving    当下时刻正在发生的动作。
（1）was/were + Ving    过去某时刻正在发生的动作。

例句：I am studying English.  【 am + studying = 时 + 态 】
例句：I was studying English at 7 pm yesterday.
例句：You were studying English then. 你当时正在学英语。

肯定句（现在）：I am crying.
肯定句（过去）：I was crying then.
否定句（现在）：I am not crying.【主语+be动词+not+Ving+其他】
否定句（过去）：I was not crying just now. 我刚才没哭.
一般疑问句（现在）：Are you crying?【be动词+主语+Ving+其他】
一般疑问句（过去）：Were you crying then?
特殊疑问句（现在）：What are you crying for?
特殊疑问句（过去）：What were you crying for?



## 主干-时态-将来时


现在将来时 ---> will/shall + be/V
过去将来时 ---> would/should + be/V

例句：I will be a teacher.  我将成为一名老师（起点是从现在开始算起）
例句：He promised that he would buy a cat for me. 他当时曾承诺他将给我买一只猫。（起点是从过去的某时刻算起）

现在将来时 ---> am/is/are going to + be/V
过去将来时 ---> was/were going to + be/V


## 主干-时态-完成时


现在完成时：have/has + 动词过去分词
- 实义动词：have/has + Ved（have/has作为助动词，翻译为已经）
- 非实义动词：have/has + been
过去完成时：两个过去肯定有一个完成
- 过去完成时：had + been\
- 过去完成时：had + Ved


例句：I have been a teacher for ten years. \
例句：I was a teacher for ten years. \
我已经是一名老师10年了. (现在还是)。我曾当过10年老师。(现在不是)

例句：I have done the work for two hours.\
例句：I did the work for two hours.\
我已经做工作两个小时了。（现在还在做）
我曾做了两个小时的工作。（现在没做了）

例句：We have been good friends for two years.\
例句：We were good friends for two years.\
我们已经是两年的好朋友了。（现在还是）
我们曾是两年的好朋友。（现在不是了）

例句：She has been away for two years.\
例句：She was away for two years.\
她已经离开2年了。（away表示状态，现在还是离开的状态）
她曾离开过2年。（表示她现在没离开）



例子：昨天早上，我在吃油条之前已经在喝豆浆了。

例子：I had been good friends with Sam before I met Tom.\
我在见到Sam之前，我就已经和Sam是好朋友了。

例子：He had been in the school for many years before I came.\
在我来之前他就已经在这个学校很多年了。

例子：By the end of last week she had written two books.\
到上周末，她已经写了两本书。

## 修饰-从句

当疑问词作主语时，则无需变更语序

独立句子: What's your name?
变成从句：I don't know what your name is.

独立句子: Why are you laughing?
变成从句：I don't know why you are laughing.



## 修饰-非谓语动词

- 非谓语动词：动名词、分词、不定式
- 动名词
- 分词
- 不定式（可作为名词、形容词、副词，不一定是什么词性的一个东西）
  - 使役动词、感官动词后续的不定式需要省略to，因为已经确定要发生了
    - He made me stay for the night.
    - She watched Michael enter the room.

on  the  bus.  在公交车上.

## AEIOU发音区分

中文 —— 象形文字，都是三点水，但发音不同，所以中文每个字都要知道怎么读。
英文 —— 每一个单词都有一个发音

a e i o u  —— 英文只要知道区分这5个字母的发音即可，其他和汉语拼音一样
mad 只有一个a，所以读【骂的】，made有a和e，所以读【妹的】

## 区分make和do

1、无中生有就用make，本来就有do

    make dinner. 做晚饭.
    do the dishes. 洗碗.

## 区分四个也的用法


    I like the shovel, too. 我也喜欢这个铲子. 句尾有逗号用too.
    I like the shovel as well. 句尾无逗号用as well. 否定用either直接替换.
    I also like the shovel. 句子中间(主谓之间)用also.
    I don't like this shovel either. 我也不喜欢这个铲子. 否定在句尾用either.


## 区分时间介词in和on和at


大于1天就用in：in summer. 在夏天;  in July. 在七月份.
等于1天就用on：on Sunday.
小于1天就用at：at ten o'clock;  at night.


三个例外:
in the morning.
in the afternoon.
in the evening.
on the weekend. 在周末.

## 区分fewlittle

区分 few、a few、little、a little

先学会分清few和little，

few —— 一只手数的过来 —— 可数，有a表示够，无a表示不够。
little —— 一只手数不过来 —— 不可数，有a表示够，无a表示不够。


I have a little money. So I can buy a candy. 
I have little money. So I can not buy a plane. 


## 区分dont和didnt

don't —— 翻译成不
didn't —— 翻译成没

I don't like studying. 我不喜欢学习.
I didn't write my homework. 我没写作业.



## 区分wouldyoulike和doyoulike


would you like —— 表示你想不想要
do you like —— 表示你喜不喜欢

Nice view! Would you like to take a look? 你想不想也看看？
Do you like this view? 你喜不喜欢这个景色？


## 区分youandI和youandme

能用 we 的时候，就用you and I
能用 us 的时候，就用you and me

we=主格=you and I
us=宾格=you and me

We love these binoculars. 我们都喜欢这望远镜.
You and I love these binoculars.

These binoculars are for us. 这望远镜是给我们准备的.
These binoculars are for you and me.

## 区分ifIwas和ifIwere

有可能性就用was，没有可能性就用were

Sorry, if I was wrong. 如果我错了，那就对不起.
If I were a camel, I would never be thirsty. 我要是一只骆驼的话，我就不会口渴。


## 区分madeof和madefrom

能看出来材料的就用of，看不出来材料的就用from

This hammer is made of wood. 这个锤子是用木头制作的.
Paper is made from trees. 纸是用木材做的。


## 区分在树上

in the tree 还是 on the tree

长在树上就用on，比如: The apple is on the tree.  苹果在树上.
没长在树上用in，比如: The bird is in the tree. 小鸟在树上.



## 区分no和not

名词前用no，其他词前用not，no紧跟名词，not紧跟的不是名词。

I have no money. 我没钱. money是名词.

There's not any money. 这里not紧跟any，而不是money.

I do not have money. 这里not紧跟have，而不是money.

## 区分四个我

I me my mine

主格——用I，宾格——me，
my + 名词，mine后面不能加名词，

This is my pump. 这是我的打气筒.
This pump is mine. 这个打气筒是我的.

## 区分有the和无the

1、有the表示地点（or其他事物），无the表示动作

go to the school. 去学校.
go to school. 去上学.



## 区分go后面到底to不to

去这儿不吐，go here.
去那儿不吐，go there.
回家不吐，go home.
去锅外不吐，go abroad.
其他地方，随便吐。(具体加to，泛指不加to)，地点副词不吐，名词要吐，









## 区分四个大

big —— 指的是尺寸体积大


heavy rain —— 大雨，雨量大
strong wind —— 大风，风很强劲
thick fog —— 大雾，雾很浓密

## 区分cancouldwould

can —— 表示能力
Can you drive? 你能开车吗？

could —— 表示有可能
Could you give me some water?  你有没有可能给我一杯水？

would —— 表示愿意
Would you like to come with me? 你愿意跟我一起来吗？


## 区分太多了

too many
too much
much too

不看前面，只看后面，many是可数，too是太的意思
too many mistakes. 可数名词mistakes.

You give too much homework. 作业不可数名词



## 区分三个说

tell、say、speak

tell + 人：I will tell the teacher! 我要告老师!
say + 内容：I want to say that I love my dog. 我想说我爱我的狗.
speak + 语言：I can speak German. 我会说德语.


say something in German.


## 区分非谓语动词todo与doing区分

（01）to + V 表示将发生或者没发生
（02）V + ing 表示发生过了(过去)

    I remember swimming last summer. 我记得去年夏天游泳了.

## IELTS WRITE RIGHT

Introduction
IELTS Write Right is intended to assist you, the IELTS candidate, in expanding your repertoire of sentence structures, improving your coherence and cohension and extending your range of vocabulary with a view to achieving better results in the IELTS writing section. By providing alternate model answers to IELTS wrting tasks, it provides a framework within which you can discover for yourself many of the important stylistic differences between everyday English and English as it is used in academic writing.

IELTS Band Scores
IELTS is designed to test the English ability of non-native speakers. However, the model answers in this book are written by a native speaker, with none of the mistakes that an IELTS candidate might make. Therefore, it is very difficult to evaluate the quality of the model answers in this book using IELTS band scores. It is important that you remember the IELTS band scores in this text are included as a general guide only and are simply intended to help you to identify better ways of writing.

Model Answers
Two model answers are provided for every question in this book. Compare them and try to find different (and more stylistically complex) ways to write. Remember that easy is not always best. In each case Model Answer A is easier to understand and will show you how to organise your essay; however, Model Answer B is an example of better writing. Therefore, you should try to base your own writing on Model Answer B.

Model Sentence Structures
To successed in IELTS writing you must learn to write complex and varied sentence structures. The "Model Sentence Structures" sections included after each pair of model answers will help you to do this. These sections demonstrate how to convert a simple sentence into academic English.
